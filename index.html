About me

You might know me from TRIA.os, an amazing ROBLOX game I enjoy a lot, I've been stuck in this hell hole of making maps for ROBLOX flood escaping games for 3 years now, starting from Flood Escape 2 back in 2020 where I really got into Roblox Studio, and learnt the basics of building and scripting. Due to poor motivation, I did not release any real maps in this game despite having started dozens of maps.

In march 2021, I moved on to making maps for TRIA.os because of Roblox's new audio update which made audios only usable in the experiences they were published to, TRIA.os made a system to get around that by manually verifying audios submitted in a special channel in their Discord server, which is why I moved to TRIA.os.

At the start of 2022, I had joined the Nightlife Devs and participated in the creation of EndZone with Marchionne, Kris, Alex28901, Literal_Cow and MalachiLeapYear, which took over 7 months to create and is currently one of the most difficult TRIA.os maps at the moment, this is where I started to get known around the community. I continued to create and help creating maps from mid 2022 to start of 2023, which you might have heard of: Reaver, The Sin, Moonlight Cosmos, Vivid, Vacuus and Dystopian World, collaborating with big creators such as Enszo, Svilar and more.

At the moment I am currently trying to make a game while also trying all sorts of stuff just like making a website.

Simple data store system
local DataStoreService = game:GetService("DataStoreService")
local Players = game:GetService("Players")
local DataStore = DataStoreService:GetDataStore("Players")
                        
local data = {}
                        
local defaultData = {
    someValue = true,
    someOtherValue = 10
}
                        
Players.PlayerAdded:Connect(function(player)
    local success, playerData = pcall(DataStore.GetAsync, DataStore, player.UserId)
                        
    if success then
        data[player.UserId] = playerData or defaultData
    else
        warn("the diarrh√©e foudroyante has been awaken")
    end
end
                        
Players.PlayerRemoving:Connect(function(player)
    DataStore:SetAsync(player.UserId, data[player.UserId]
    data[player.UserId] = nil
end)
                        
game:BindToClose(function()
    for _, player in pairs(Players:GetPlayers())
        pcall(DataStore.SetAsync, DataStore, player.UserId, data[player.UserId])
    end
end)
                        
while true do
    task.wait(200)
                        
    for _, player in pairs(Players:GetPlayers())
        pcall(DataStore.SetAsync, DataStore, player.UserId, data[player.UserId])
    end
end
